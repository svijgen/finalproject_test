Education

We specialize in educating children experiencing trauma and supporting families in achieving long-term stability. Children experience trauma due to homelessness, poverty, abuse, witnessing abuse, separation from a parent, and crises within the family. Due to trauma and additional obstacles, many children come to First Place up to three grade levels behind their peers.
Our staff is highly trained in recognizing the individual needs of children experiencing trauma and are ready to provide the support they need to continue learning and growing. We know it takes more than an education and a place to live to make this a reality. We focus on working with each individual child and family unit in their personal journey. Each part of our education program is designed to help students successfully transition into their next school and have the skills they need to graduate junior high, then high school, then college and become successful, productive adults.
