Giving

Your support helps children and families who are struggling with the effects of poverty and crisis. With your help, First Place is breaking the cycle of poverty in our community. First Place is a 501(c)3 non-profit organization. Our Federal EIN is 94-3092447. We have various ways of supporting our programs. Please browse below to see all of the ways you can help.

Donate Online:

Our PayPal is set-up for you to make a secure donation to First Place with any major credit card.

By Check:

Make your check out to First Place and mail it to: First Place Development Office, PO Box 22536, SEattle, WA., 98122.

By Phone:

We accept all major credit cards by phone. To make a credit card donation, please call (206)323-6715.

Memorial or Tribute Gifts:

Philanthropic giving is a lovely way to honor the memory of a loved one. You may also wish to make a Tribute gift to makr a special occasion, such as a birthday or anniversary, in the life of someone who is important to you.
If you wish to make a gift in memory or in honor of an individual, please donate by phone, online or with a check as indicated above. you should also email the following information to our Development office at development@firstplaceschool.org:

  • •Name of the individual(s) being honored
  • •Whether this is a tribute or a memorial gift (if it is a tribute, please note the occasion)
  • •The name and contact information of the person whom we should contact, letting them know of your donation

From there, we send an occasion-appropriate card noting your gift (excluding the donation amount) and we send you a letter of thanks that you can use for charitable donation reporting. If you have questions, please call the Developmennt office.

Bequests, Planned Giving, Gifts of Stock, Endowments:

First Place Development Staff can work with you individually on how you woul dlike to make a donation. To learn more or inquire about adding First Place into your will, gifts of stock and our endowment, please call us at (206)323-6715.

Major Gifts, $1,000+:

If you are currently a major donor to First Place, or are considering investing in our organization, please call our Development department to discuss or schedule an individual giving plan consultation.
